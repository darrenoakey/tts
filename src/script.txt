# Voice Training Script (2 minutes)
# Phonetically balanced text covering English phonemes
# Reading time: approximately 2 minutes at normal pace

When the sunlight strikes raindrops in the air, they act as a prism and form a rainbow. The rainbow is a division of white light into many beautiful colors. These take the shape of a long round arch, with its path high above, and its two ends apparently beyond the horizon.

You wished to know all about my grandfather. Well, he is nearly ninety-three years old. He dresses himself in an ancient black frock coat, usually minus several buttons; yet he still thinks as swiftly as ever. A long, flowing beard clings to his chin, giving those who observe him a pronounced feeling of the utmost respect.

The birch canoe slid smoothly on the calm lake surface. A thick fog crept over the jagged mountain peaks at dawn. She held the heavy bronze jar with both hands, feeling its cool weight. The quick brown fox jumped lazily over the sleeping dog.

How wonderful! The garden was absolutely magnificent this morning. Did you notice the butterflies dancing among the flowers? Wait, what was that noise? Perhaps it was just the wind rustling through the leaves.

She sells seashells by the seashore. Peter Piper picked a peck of pickled peppers. The sixth sick sheik's sixth sheep is sick. Strength and strategy are essential for success.

The weather whether we like it or not will change with the seasons. Through thick and thin, through trials and tribulations, we persevere. What we do today echoes through eternity, shaping tomorrow's world.
